How to use Group Members Botter

Steps for using Group Members Botter:

  • Press the button that says "Open GroupApi". Once done go to inspect element console and head over to the tab labeled "network". Once here click "ctrl" and "r" or refresh your page manually.
  • Scroll up until you see "groupmembers". Right click on "groupmembers" and hover over copy, a bunch of options will pop up go ahead and select "Copy all as Powershell" or "Powershell" Once done go back to this website paste the code you copied from the GroupApi page into the "Group Code" Box and then copy the id of the group you want to bot and paste it into "Group ID" box. Then Click The "Start Botter" Button. Wait until the bar finishes loading and then the Botter will start!
